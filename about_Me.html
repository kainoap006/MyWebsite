About Me

I decided to study computer science at UCSD because the college was in my hometown and I've always wanted to satisfy my curiousity about technology. I used to take remotes or toys apart when I was a child to see what was inside. This led to me joining my elementary school's robotics team where I was introduced to programming. At first it was just Legos but after 10 years I was working on actual robots and creating new devices for fun. In high school, I took a plethora of engineering / computer science classes which my school luckily had at the time and discovered how important software was. From VR to AI to the next buzz item such as NFTs or Bitcoin there was always something garnering hype surrounding software and I wanted to learn more about them. So I studied computer science and now I am learning about careers in this field. Meeting some of the most interesting people who have worked on games or started a company. There is so much more to explore and I am excited to put myself out there and find out.
